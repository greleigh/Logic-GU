Journées sur les Arithmétiques Faibles 33

The 33rd meeting of JAF (Journées sur les Arithmétiques Faibles) was held in Gothenburg, Sweden, June 16-18 2014.

Programme

A detailed programme can be found here: JAF33 Programme.

Scope

  • Provability and Definability in Arithmetics
  • Model Theory and Arithmetics
  • (Un)decidability of Arithmetics
  • Modelling computations on Logical Theories

Invited speakers

  • Lev Beklemishev (Steklov Institute) Arithmetic based on logic without contraction and Gödel’s second incompleteness theorem (abstract, slides)
  • Paola D’Aquino (Seconda Universitá di Napoli) Recursive saturation from a valuation theoretic point of view (abstract, slides)
  • Costas Dimitracopoulos (University of Athens) End extensions of models (joint work with V. Paschalis) (abstract, slides)
  • Fernando Ferreira (University of Lisbon) Impredicativity and Fregean Arithmetic (abstract, slides)
  • Leszek Kołodziejczyk (University of Warsaw) The problem of a model with no collection and no exponentiation (abstract, slides)
  • James Schmerl (University of Connecticut) Submodel lattices of Nerode semirings (abstract, slides)
  • Keita Yokoyama (Japan Advanced Institute of Science And Technology) Use of nonstandard models in reverse mathematics (abstract, slides)

Tutorial

  • Fernando Ferreira (University of Lisbon) Feasible Analysis (abstract, slides1, slides2)

Contributed talks

  • Henri-Alex Esbelin (Blaise Pascal University) Counting in Δ0#N modulo matrices monoids (abstract, slides)
  • Andrés Cordón-Franco and Francisco Félix Lara-Martín (University of Seville) Local induction axioms vs local induction rules (abstract, slides)
  • Yoann Marquer (University Paris-Est Créteil) Algorithmic completeness of imperative languages (abstract, slides)
  • Tin Lok Wong (Kurt Gödel Research Center) Understanding BΣ1 + exp via WKL0* (abstract, slides)
  • Rasmus Blanck (University of Gothenburg) Flexible formulae of arithmetic (abstract, slides)
